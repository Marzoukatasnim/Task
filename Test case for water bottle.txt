Test case for a non-plastic water bottle:
1. Check if the cap tightly lock the water bottle so that there is no water dripping while the cap is locked 
2.Check if there is any leakage in the bottle body while the bottle is filled with water
3.check the width/diameter of the bottle to ensure that it is easily grippable
4.Check if the bottom part is equally even or not to ensure that the bottle can stand straight
5.Check if the holder can hold the bottle when it is filled  with water
6.Check if the bottle shrink or expand when the bottle is filled with specific temparature of water
7.Check if the bottle has a narrow mouth or not
